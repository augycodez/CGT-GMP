CDSCO (India) — Key Guidance

Selected CDSCO guidance documents and frameworks relevant to advanced therapies and clinical development in India.

  • NDC Guidelines — National Drug Control (NDC) guidance covering regulatory standards for drug development and manufacturing. Check the CDSCO portal for official documents and updates.
  • Stem Cell: New Drugs and Clinical Trials Rules (2019) — Regulatory framework for clinical trials, manufacture and use of stem cell-based products and biologics in India.
  • ATMP Framework — Guidance and regulatory expectations for Advanced Therapy Medicinal Products (ATMPs) including quality, non-clinical and clinical considerations.

For full texts and latest versions, consult the official CDSCO website and notifications.
